Damage Prevention

Commercial Water Damage Risk Assessment and Prevention

Commercial property owners have a duty of care to maintain safe conditions on their properties. Hidden moisture, aging plumbing, and inadequate waterproofing can result in sudden failures that cause injuries, property damage, and costly lawsuits. Wichita Restoration Co provides professional water damage risk assessments using moisture monitoring equipment and thermal imaging technology that evaluate every area's vulnerability to water intrusion. Our written reports identify risks, assign priority levels, and recommend specific preventive actions.

These assessments serve a dual purpose: they protect people and property by identifying vulnerabilities, and they demonstrate due diligence that can protect you legally if an incident occurs. Courts and insurance companies look favorably on property owners who maintain regular professional inspections and act on expert recommendations. We recommend annual moisture assessments for all commercial properties, with more frequent inspections for high-risk areas such as basements, mechanical rooms, and aging plumbing systems.
